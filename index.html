POO SNAKE

snake number 2!

Controls:

  • Mobile: tap the top/bottom half to go up/down while moving left/right.
  • Tap left/right half to go left/right while moving up/down.
  • Keyboard arrow keys also work on desktop.

Magic poos:

  • Yellow slows you down.
  • Green halves your length.
  • White gives god mode until the next level.
  • Blue gives you a pet fly that eats poos.

Glowing poos only stay magic for a few seconds. The fly stays until you eat it.

Tap the canvas to control the snake. Keyboard arrows work too.
